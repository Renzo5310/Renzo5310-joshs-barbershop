Meet Josh

I’m Josh, an award-winning professional barber based in Bellingham, WA. I’ve spent years refining my craft, blending street culture with luxury-level precision. I specialize in clean fades, sharp lineups, detailed beard work, and tailored grooming designed to elevate your look, not just maintain it.

Every cut is intentional. No rushed sessions, no shortcuts — just focus, detail, and consistency. My chair is a space where confidence gets built and style gets upgraded. Whether it’s a subtle refresh or a full transformation, I make sure every client leaves polished, respected, and moving different. Street roots. Luxury standards.

This isn’t just barbering — it’s a statement. My goal is to make sure every client leaves feeling confident and fresh.
